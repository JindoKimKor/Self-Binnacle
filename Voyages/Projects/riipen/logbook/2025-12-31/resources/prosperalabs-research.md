## What They Do

Prospera Labs는 **AI-first CRM 및 Agent Platform**을 개발하는 스타트업. 핵심 제품은 **Prospera CCM (Customer Conversation Manager)**.

### Core Product
- AI Agent platform for customer journey automation
- From initial inquiries → qualifications → proposals → deal closure → customer support

### Key Products
1. **AI Receptionist** - 24/7 phone call handling, scheduling, reminders
2. **AI Sales Agent** - Client interactions, project requirements gathering, proposal generation
3. **WordFactory** - Content creation tool for social media agencies
