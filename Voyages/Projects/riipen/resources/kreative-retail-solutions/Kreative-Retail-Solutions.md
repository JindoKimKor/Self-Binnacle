## Project Details

**Categories:** Information Technology, Project Management, Software Development, Website Development

### Description

Kreative Retail Solutions is seeking to enhance its online presence by integrating its website with its Point of Sale (POS) system and additional third-party platforms. The current website is out of sync with the POS (Lightspeed), leading to inefficiencies in inventory management and sales tracking.

Focus areas:
- Syncing website with POS system (Lightspeed) for real-time stock levels and sales data
- Linking website to third-party platforms to expand reach and improve customer engagement
- Creating a seamless, automated process for operational efficiency and customer satisfaction

### Deliverables

1. Fully integrated website syncing with the existing POS system and connected to specified third-party platforms
2. Documentation detailing the integration process and any code modifications
3. Testing report demonstrating functionality and reliability of integrations
4. Presentation summarizing project outcomes and potential areas for future improvement
